What is a Alpha Decay

In alpha decay, an alpha particle, consisting of 2 protons and 2 neutrons, is ejected from the atomic nucleus. This leads to a decrease in atomic mass and atomic number.

What is a Beta Decay?

A neutron in the nucleus is converted into a proton, an electron, and an antineutrino. The electron is ejected from the nucleus, increasing the atomic number by 1.
